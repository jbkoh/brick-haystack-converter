ph::Ref

Reference to an Entity. Ref values are ASCII strings limited to the following chars: a-z, A-Z, 0-9, underbar, colon, dash, dot, or tilde. Refs may be parameterized with a referent Dict kind name and direction as "<<" or ">>".
